Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) Policy

Last Updated: December 14, 2025

InvenTalk is committed to protecting children and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) on our platform. This policy outlines our comprehensive approach to child safety.

1. Our Commitment

InvenTalk has zero tolerance for any content, behavior, or activity that exploits, harms, or endangers children. We are dedicated to creating a safe environment for all users, especially minors, and actively work to prevent, detect, and remove any CSAE-related content or behavior.

2. Prohibited Content and Behavior

The following are strictly prohibited on InvenTalk:

  • Any content that depicts, promotes, or facilitates child sexual abuse or exploitation
  • Sexualized content involving minors
  • Grooming behaviors or attempts to establish inappropriate relationships with minors
  • Sharing, requesting, or distributing child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Any communication that sexualizes or targets minors
  • Content that normalizes or encourages child exploitation

3. Age Verification and Protection

3.1 User Registration

  • Users must be at least 13 years old to create an account (or the minimum age required by local law)
  • We implement age verification measures during registration
  • Users under 18 are considered minors and receive additional protections

3.2 Age-Appropriate Features

  • Certain features may be restricted for users under 18
  • We provide parental controls and safety settings
  • Minors are protected from inappropriate content and interactions

4. Content Moderation

4.1 Automated Detection

  • We use advanced technology and AI-powered tools to detect and remove CSAE-related content
  • Automated systems scan user-generated content, messages, and media uploads
  • Suspicious patterns and behaviors are flagged for human review

4.2 Human Review

  • Trained content moderators review reported content and flagged material
  • Reports are prioritized and reviewed within 24 hours
  • Expert review is conducted for complex cases

4.3 User Reporting

  • Users can report suspicious content, behavior, or accounts through in-app reporting tools
  • Reports are confidential and can be made anonymously
  • We encourage users to report any content or behavior that may endanger children

5. Response and Enforcement

5.1 Immediate Actions

  • Any confirmed CSAE content is immediately removed
  • Accounts involved in CSAE activities are permanently banned
  • We preserve evidence for law enforcement when required

5.2 Law Enforcement Cooperation

  • We report all suspected CSAE content to relevant authorities, including the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and local law enforcement
  • We fully cooperate with law enforcement investigations
  • We maintain records and evidence as required by law

5.3 Account Termination

  • Users who violate this policy face immediate and permanent account termination
  • We may take legal action against violators
  • Device and IP-based blocking may be implemented to prevent re-registration

6. Safety Features

  • Privacy Controls: Users can control who can contact them and view their content
  • Blocking and Reporting: Easy-to-use tools to block and report inappropriate users
  • Content Filtering: Automated filtering of inappropriate content
  • Safe Messaging: Monitoring and filtering of direct messages
  • Parental Controls: Features for parents to monitor and control their children's usage

7. Education and Awareness

  • We provide educational resources about online safety for users and parents
  • Regular safety tips and guidelines are shared within the app
  • We collaborate with child safety organizations to promote awareness

8. Compliance and Standards

InvenTalk complies with:

  • Google Play's Child Safety Standards Policy
  • Apple App Store's Child Safety Guidelines
  • International laws and regulations regarding child protection
  • Industry best practices for child safety online

9. Continuous Improvement

  • We regularly review and update our safety policies and procedures
  • We invest in new technologies and tools to improve detection and prevention
  • We conduct regular training for our moderation team
  • We stay informed about emerging threats and adapt our measures accordingly

10. Transparency

  • We publish regular transparency reports about our safety efforts
  • We are transparent about our moderation processes and policies
  • We engage with child safety experts and organizations for guidance

Reporting and Contact

If you encounter any content or behavior that violates this policy, please report it immediately through:

  • In-App Reporting: Use the report feature within the InvenTalk app
  • Email: safety@inventalk.com

For emergency situations involving immediate danger to a child, please contact your local law enforcement immediately.

National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC):
Website: www.missingkids.org
CyberTipline: www.missingkids.org/gethelpnow/cybertipline

11. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. Users will be notified of significant changes through the app or via email. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.

12. Acknowledgment

By using InvenTalk, users acknowledge that they have read, understood, and agree to comply with this CSAE Policy. Violation of this policy will result in immediate account termination and may result in legal action.
